General Khalid bin Walid (RA)

The Sword of Allah - A Legend in Islamic Military History

Biography

Khalid bin Walid (RA), known as the Sword of Allah, was a key military commander during the time of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). He converted to Islam after the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah and went on to lead the Muslim armies to numerous victories across Arabia, Persia, and the Byzantine Empire.

Battles

  • Battle of Mu'tah
  • Battle of Yamama
  • Battle of Yarmouk
  • Battle of Qadisiyyah
  • Battle of Firaz

Famous Quotes

"I am the sword of Allah, and it is through me that Allah will bring victory."
"If you were in the clouds, Allah would raise us to fight you."
